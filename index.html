Synthesize Your Framework

Welcome to the Synthesizer. This is where you bring together the insights you've gathered from the B.R.O.A. (Beliefs, Rules, Ontology, Authenticity) explorers.

How to Use This Tool:

  • Upload your .txt files from each explorer. You can upload multiple files into the same box.
  • Edit the text directly in the display boxes to remove outdated ideas or add new notes.
  • If you want to start over, use the 'X' button to clear an entire box.
  • (Optional) Use the AI Analyzer to get feedback and suggestions to help you write your synthesis.
  • Write your synthesis, reflecting on how these different parts of yourself work together.
  • Download your complete PRF as a single file to save your work.
